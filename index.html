Who we are



Steeleson fabrications is a metalwork fabrication & design company located in the North West. We’re able to offer a complete service from initial concept & consultation with our design & engineering team through to fully manufactured & finished products for use in all Industrial, Residential & Commercial environments. Our experienced team undertake all types of projects ranging from bespoke fabrications to small production runs tailoring each project to suit our customer's needs & providing the complete finished product to the highest of standards. We work with a wide range of different steels & can offer a variety of high-level finishes using some of the best products on the market. Our services include, CAD design & Engineering, TIG/MIG welding, Forming, Laser Cutting, CNC Machining, Polishing, Powder Coating & more….
